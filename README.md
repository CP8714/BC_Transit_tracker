Welcome to the BCTVicTracker

A Bus Tracking and Next Bus tool for BC Transit in Victoria, BC also known as the Victoria Regional Transit System

This website allows users to track a specific BC Transit bus running in Victoria like 9542 and get info such as how early/late it is, how busy it is, what route it is running, and the estimated arrival times at every stop on its current trip. Users can also look up the estimated arrival times
